Create an account

  1. Navigate to the Sign Up button in top right corner of your screen and click it.

  2. Fill out the form presented until no errors are shown and then proceed by clicking Sign Up at the bottom of the form

  3. If everything went as expected you should now be logged in and instead of a Log In and Sign Up button your account name should be displayed in the navbar.

View Other Streams

Navigate back to the Browse page and click on your favourite game, all live streamers that are playing that game will be listed there. Click on one of them and enjoy the show!

Follow Other Streams

Once you have navigated to an existing channel, all you have to do is click the Follow button that is displayed above the video stream. Once you have followed a user you will now easily find their stream from the browsing page if they are live.

Start Streaming

  1. Create an account by pressing the Sign Up button in the navbar and then proceed to create an account.

  2. Once logged in you can navigate to your Stream Settings by clicking on your name in the top right corner, followed by settings.

  3. Press the Fetch Key button to generate your secret unique streamkey.

  4. Install any third party software used to stream. We recommend that you install OBS.

  5. Make sure that the stream url in your third party streaming software is set to: rtmp://localhost/live and copy your unique streamkey from our website to paste it into the streamkey field of your software.

  6. Before you start streaming, make sure to change your Live Settings which also can be located in the settings page.

  7. Once you have started the stream you can view it by navigating to your Channel by first clicking your name in the top right corner, followed by channel.
